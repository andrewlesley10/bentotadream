About Us

Your Trusted Hotel Supply & Procurement Partner

Based in Beruwala, we serve hotels and resorts across Sri Lanka with quality-first procurement and setup solutions.

Bentota Dream Holding (Private) Limited is a leading hospitality procurement company. Founded with a vision to simplify hotel sourcing in challenging terrains, we bring consistency, quality, and reliability to every hospitality project we undertake.

Over the years, we’ve built a reputation as a trusted supply partner for some of the region’s most respected hotels — from boutique villas to large-scale resort groups. Our strength lies in understanding the unique needs of island hospitality and delivering tailored, end-to-end procurement solutions with precision.

What We Do

We provide end-to-end supply solutions for:

  • Guest Rooms – bed & bath linen, in-room amenities, appliances
  • Housekeeping – cleaning tools, trolley systems, chemicals
  • Food & Beverage – kitchenware, buffet and banquet items
  • Property Setup Projects – full-room setup for new hotel launches

At Bentota Dream, we don't just deliver products — we deliver trust, efficiency, and peace of mind.

Whether you're opening a new hotel or scaling an existing one, we're here to make your supply chain hassle-free and future-ready.

Every item we supply can be fully customized with bespoke branding, sizing, and designs to reflect your property's unique vision.
