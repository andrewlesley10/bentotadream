About Us

Your Dependable Sri Lankan Hospitality Procurement Partner

Setting the Standard in Hotel Supply

Bentota Dream Holding (Private) Limited is a premier Sri Lanka-based hospitality supply and procurement company dedicated to serving hotels, resorts, villas, and guesthouses with dependable sourcing and high-quality hotel essentials.

Understanding the demanding nature of the hospitality landscape, we bridge the gap between quality manufacturing and daily hotel operations. Our extensive network and stringent quality control ensure that properties across the island receive products that not only meet but exceed the expectations of their sophisticated guests.

From essential housekeeping tools and luxurious bed linen to beautifully crafted dining ware, we pride ourselves on delivering solutions that elevate the guest experience while providing robust durability and value to property owners.
